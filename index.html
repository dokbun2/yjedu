A piece of Danish design history

Carl Hansen & Søn represents over 100 years of Danish design history and is the brand behind some of the most well-known pieces found in luxury homes, five-star hotels, restaurants and cafés around the world. Known for their raw materials, timeless design and excellent craftsmanship, all pieces are still made at their factory in Denmark today.

Get to know Ethnicraft

One of Spruce's main brands hails from Belgium. Founded more than 25 years ago, Ethnicraft's solid wood furniture can blend seamlessly into any room. The brand prides itself on its large product range, which covers sofas, dining sets, coffee tables, side tables, beds, desks and chairs. All designed to withstand time with its simple aesthetic, functionality and durability.
