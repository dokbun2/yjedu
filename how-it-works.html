Common Questions

What's the minimum subscription period?
Our minimum subscription period is 6 months. After that, you can continue month-to-month, swap items, or cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
What happens if furniture gets damaged?
Normal wear and tear is completely covered. For damage beyond normal use, we'll assess on a case-by-case basis. We're very reasonable and want you to live comfortably with our furniture.
Can I buy the furniture later?
Yes! If you fall in love with a piece, you can purchase it. We'll credit a portion of your rental payments toward the purchase price.
What areas do you deliver to?
We currently deliver throughout Bangkok and surrounding areas including Nonthaburi, Pathum Thani, and Samut Prakan. Contact us for delivery outside these areas.
How does swapping work?
After your minimum 6-month period, you can request to swap items. There's a small swap fee (‡∏ø500-1,000) to cover logistics. We'll arrange pickup of old items and delivery of new ones.
Is furniture new or used?
All furniture is professionally cleaned and inspected before delivery. Some items may have been previously rented, but all are in excellent condition. We maintain the highest quality standards.
